Marketing Campaign Analysis
in Power BI

For this marketing campaign analysis, I grouped the metrics into three main categories: Awareness, Engagement, and Performance.
Awareness: Focuses on gauging the campaign's reach and visibility. Key metrics analyzed include impressions, cost per thousand (CPM), and conversion rate. Engagement: Evaluates how the audience interacts with the ad. This includes tracking metrics like likes, shares, comments, clicks, and click-through rate (CTR).
Performance: Measures the campaign's overall effectiveness and financial outcomes..

Financial Income Statement Analysis
in Power BI

I’m passionate about financial analysis, especially working with income statements. After participating in the Onyx data challenges, I was inspired by Iris’s report, which won the Onyx Data ZoomChart mini challenge in July 2024. I loved her work so much that I decided to recreate it, making some changes by adding new features and leaving out a few. Key features include: • Dynamic KPIs: Users can easily switch between different metrics, such as OPEX, EBIT, Gross Profit, and Net Profit. • Dynamic Titles: Users can quickly see their selected metric (KPI). • Dynamic Colors: Each selected metric is highlighted with unique colors for easy differentiation. • Income Statement: Users can view how well the business strategies performed during the financial period. .

Sports Product Sales Report
in SQL and Power BI

In this project, I will conduct a comprehensive sales analysis of sports products. With the increasing number of sports brands entering the market, analyzing sales data is crucial for providing valuable insights to investors, helping them stay competitive and identify market trends. Sports equipment, also known as sporting goods, includes tools, materials, apparel, and gear used in various sports, ranging from balls and nets to protective items like helmets. These products play a key role in both enhancing athletic performance and ensuring safety. Over time, the evolution of sports has led to an increased focus on protective gear to prevent injuries. While sporting equipment can be found in department stores and specialized shops, this project specifically analyzes data from six retail stores in the United States.
